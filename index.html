AutoToy: Self-Driving Toy Car

An innovative DIY project combining robotics, computer vision, and machine learning to create a fully autonomous toy car that can navigate obstacles, follow paths, and interact with its environment. Powered by Raspberry Pi and Arduino, this project showcases practical applications of artificial intelligence in robotics.
